Offshape is a creative studio founded by German fashion designer Oliver Schraft. Our ambition is to weave narratives that transcend conventional fashion collections, crafting a modern folklore where myths are spread through ethernet cables. Inspired by the immersive worlds of video games, we merge garments and avatars, creating looks where the lines blur between outfit and identity. Each collection tells a unique story, inviting you to step into a realm where fashion and fantasy are seamlessly intertwined.
