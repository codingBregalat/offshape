Offshape is a creative studio founded by German fashion designer Oliver Schraft. Our ambition is to weave narratives that transcend conventional fashion collections. Inspired by the immersive worlds of digital media, we go on a quest to find silhouettes that playfully shape and abstract the human form, and develop garments that bridge the gap between avant-garde and prêt-à-porter. Each collection tells a unique story, inviting you to step into a realm where fashion and fantasy are seamlessly intertwined.
