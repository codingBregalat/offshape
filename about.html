Offshape is a creative studio founded by me, Oliver Schraft, a digital fashion designer and image creator from Germany. This space serves as the outlet for my creative ideas, where I draw inspiration from animation and gamification. Inspired by the human form, my creations transform the body with exaggerated proportions and bold silhouettes. I strive to use this platform to collaborate with other creatives, combining our visions to create unique digital or physical products.
